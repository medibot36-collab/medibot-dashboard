HELP & DOCUMENTATION

🔌 Connecting to ESP32

  • Upload the firmware to your ESP32 via Arduino IDE.
  • Connect ESP32 to your local Wi-Fi network.
  • Find the IP address from the Serial Monitor.
  • Enter the IP in the top bar and click Connect.

📷 Camera Feed Setup

  • Use an ESP32-CAM module with the CameraWebServer sketch.
  • Ensure it's on the same Wi-Fi as your computer.
  • Enter its IP in the Camera Feed section and click Connect.
  • The stream will appear live in the dashboard.

❤️ Sensor Readings

  • MAX30102 sensor provides Heart Rate and SpO₂.
  • Baseline is calculated over the first 30 readings.
  • Z-score > 2 triggers a Warning, > 3 triggers Danger.
  • Click Sound Alert Buzzer to activate the buzzer on the robot.

🤖 Robot Control

  • Use arrow buttons or keyboard arrow keys to move.
  • Adjust the speed slider before sending commands.
  • Always click STOP before releasing controls.
  • Select a patient from the dropdown to navigate to their bed.
